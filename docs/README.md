# 🎓 Online Training Management Platform (OTMP)

## Project Overview

The Online Training Management Platform (OTMP) is a Salesforce-based application designed to manage online training operations. It streamlines student enrollment, course management, batch scheduling, trainer management, payments, customer support, and AI-powered assistance using Agentforce.


## Problem Statement

Training institutes often manage students, trainers, courses, and payments manually, leading to delays and errors. OTMP centralizes these processes in Salesforce to improve efficiency and provide better support.


## Business Goals

- Automate student enrollment
- Manage courses and trainers
- Track payments
- Improve customer support
- Provide AI-powered assistance using Agentforce
- Generate business reports and dashboards
